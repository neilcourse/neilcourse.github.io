crafty chocolate cake

method

  1. oven 160°C
  2. put all cake ingredients in food processor and combine until smooth, dark brown and creamy.
  3. spoon evenly into two 18cm, greased and lined sandwich tins. Cover with greaseproof paper, so they don't cook too quickly, and bake for 45 mins.
  4. remove from the oven and test by pressing the top of one gently; if it comes back with a bounce it's cooked, if it stays down it needs another five minutes. Allow to cool, then turn out of the tins.
  5. Spread one half with jam and the other with a third of the cream. Sandwich together and use the rest of the cream as a topping.

notes

 

  • ingredients

    • 175g sr flour
    • 2 heaped tbsp cocoa powder
    • 1 heaped tsp baking powder
    • 125g sugar
    • 1 dsp black treacle
    • 150ml sunflower oil
    • 150ml milk
    • 2 large eggs
    • 4 tbsp black cherry jam
    • 175ml double cream, whipped and sweetened to taste
