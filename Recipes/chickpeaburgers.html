crunchy chickpea burgers

method

  1. cook the onion in the oil until solf. Add the garlic and moroccan seasoning and cook for 1 min. Add the carrot and red pepper and cook for another 2 mins. Tip into a bowl.
  2. Mash the drained chickpeas and add to the onion mix with the breadcrumbs, almonds and herbs. Season well.
  3. Mix in the egg to bind the mixture. Line a baking tray or flat dish with baking paper. Shape the mixture into 6 burgers, put on the paper and chill for 2 hours.
  4. Barbarcue or grill the burgers for 5 mins on each side and serve with salad and pitta bread.

notes

serves 6.

ready in 35 mins plus chilling.

Moroccan Seasoning

  • 5 tsp nutmeg
  • 5 tsp cumin
  • 5 tsp gr coriander
  • 2.5 tsp all spice
  • 2.5 tsp ginger
  • 1.25 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1.25 cinnamon

 

  • ingredients

    • 1 med onion
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 garlic clove
    • 1 tbsp moroccan rub
    • 1 carrot, grated
    • 1 pointed sweet red pepper, deseeded and chopped
    • 400g can chickpeas
    • 100g fresh wholemeal breadcrumbs
    • 50g whole blanched almonds, chopped
    • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
    • 1 tbsp fresh chopped flat-leaf parsley
    • 1 med egg, beaten
    • salad and pitta to serve
