nan's fruit cake

method

  1. oven 160°C
  2. combine all ingredients
  3. cook 1.5 - 2 hours

notes

 

  • ingredients

    • 5oz sr flour
    • 5oz plain flour
    • 2 level tsp cornflour
    • .5 tsp salt
    • 6oz marg
    • 6oz sugar
    • 3 eggs
    • 1lb fruit
    • 2-3 tbsp milk
    • .25 pt water if necessary
