gingerbread men

method

  1. oven 180°C
  2. heat marj, honey, sugar gently in saucepan.
  3. sieve flour and ginger into a bowl. add marg and egg, mix to form biscuit dough.
  4. roll out on a slightly floured surface to a depth of 6mm. Cut out men and place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Place sultanas, currants or silbver balls in position to represent eyes; cut thin strips of glace cherries for mouths.
  5. bake for 12-15 mins. cool on wire rack.

notes

makes approx 10 men.

 

  • ingredients

    • 50g marg
    • 40g clear honey
    • 25g soft brown sugar
    • 225g sr flour
    • 2 tsp ginger
    • 1 egg, beaten
    • sultanas, currants, silver balls, glace cherries
