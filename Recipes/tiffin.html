tiffin

method

  1. melt together A.
  2. add B.
  3. pour into a small greased swiss roll tin & cool.
  4. melt cooking choc and spread over top.
  5. leave 2-3 hours, then cut into fingers.

notes

 

  • ingredients

    • A
    • 100g marg
    • 2 l.tbsp syrup
    • 1 dsp sugar
    • 3 dsp drinking choc
    • B
    • 225g (.5 lb) digestive bisc - crushed
    • cooking chocolate
