ginger cake

method

  1. oven 175°C
  2. sift A into bowl
  3. heat B to melt marg. stir into A with milk and beaten egg
  4. pour into greased 18cm cake tin and bake for 1 hour. cool on wire rack.

notes

 

  • ingredients

    • A
    • 8oz plain flour
    • half level tsp salt
    • 1.5 l.tsp gnd ginger
    • 1.5 l.tsp baking powder
    • .5 l.tsp bicarb of soda
    • B
    • 4oz sugar
    • 3oz marg
    • 3oz black treacle
    • 3oz syrup
    • .25 pt milk
    • 1 small egg
