f-Harmonic Maps

A non-constant f-harmonic map S2 →S2 of degree zero.
We calculate the Euler-Lagrange equation for longitutinally symmetric f-harmonic maps u : S2 → S2, and give a particular example of an f and a u which satisfy this equation.
